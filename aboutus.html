Understanding SDG-8

SDG-8 focuses on “Decent Work and Economic Growth.”

Our team deeply understood that rural areas often suffer from unemployment, lack of awareness of skills, and poor access to job opportunities. So, we built a platform that:

✔ connects rural users with jobs
✔ teaches skills
✔ gives access to schemes
✔ helps boost the rural economy

This project revolves entirely around SDG-8!
