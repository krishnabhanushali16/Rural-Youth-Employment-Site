Government Schemes for Rural Youth

Explore employment, skill development, entrepreneurship and financial support schemes designed to uplift rural communities.

Explore Key Schemes

These schemes are run by the Government of India and various ministries. Click on “Official Website” to read eligibility, benefits, and application steps.

MGNREGA – Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act

Provides at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment per year to rural households. Helps reduce distress migration and ensures basic livelihood security.

Official Website

PMKVY – Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana

Skill India’s flagship scheme offering free short-term skill development courses and certifications to make youth more employable.

Official Website

DDU-GKY – Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana

Focuses on rural poor youth aged 15–35. Offers training, placement, and long-term career support with minimum wage jobs.

Official Website

PMEGP – Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme

Provides financial assistance for setting up micro-enterprises in rural and urban areas through banks with government subsidy.

Official Website

NRLM / Aajeevika – National Rural Livelihoods Mission

Promotes self-help groups (SHGs), small businesses, and livelihood collectives especially for rural women and vulnerable communities.

Official Website

Startup India & Stand-Up India (Rural Focus)

Supports rural entrepreneurs with mentorship, market linkages, and financial support under various innovation and startup programs.
